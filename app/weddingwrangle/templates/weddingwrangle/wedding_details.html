Han Solo & Luke Skywalker’s Wedding

Welcome to the wedding details page for Han and Luke! We’re thrilled to celebrate their galactic union with you. Below, you’ll find all the details you need to make this a day to remember.

Wedding Details

Date: 12th of Life Day, Galactic Year 55

Time: Ceremony at 1600 hours, followed by reception

Venue: Cloud City, Bespin

How to Get There

Shuttle services are available from major hyperspace routes. The closest docking station is the Cloud City Landing Pad. Please confirm transport arrangements with Lando’s Shuttle Service for a smooth arrival.

Where to Stay

Here are some excellent accommodations for your stay:

  • The Bespin Grand: Luxurious suites with breathtaking views of the gas giant.
  • Ugnaught Inn: Budget-friendly but cozy, run by friendly Ugnaught hosts.
  • Falcon’s Nest: For those looking for a more adventurous stay aboard a ship.

Gift Registry

Han and Luke request contributions to their honeymoon fund as they embark on an adventure across the Outer Rim. You can make a donation via the Holonet payment system or bring credits to the event.
